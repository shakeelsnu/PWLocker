About Password Locker

This password manager securely stores your passwords and allows you to access them from any device using your mobile number and master password.

Your data is encrypted before being stored in the cloud, ensuring only you can access your passwords.

All exports are in ZIP format containing both JSON and TXT files for your convenience.
